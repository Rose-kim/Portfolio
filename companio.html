Make your lunchtime easier.

Companio suggests ways to solve problems that people have when they eat together.
After choosing a restaurant by majority decision in the group, each person can choose the food they want. Finally, individuals can pay for themselves.

Customize your preference.

Don't worry about food allergies or dietary preferences. Even in a group, you will never get recommendations you'd like to avoid.

Get customized recommendations.

Companio recommendation systems will recommend restaurants based on group member's preferences.

Make polls, and arder all in one process.

Depending on the majority decision, the system will show members menu selection and payment process.

PROBLEM

- Hard to order and split precisely as a group.
- People tend to be more indecisive when they eat together.
- Lunch time is limited in a workplace.

SOLUTION

- Order & pay separately
- Group recommendation
- Pick up / Dine in reservation

EVALUATE

I had a lot of fun designing the Companio mobile and web application and trying to solve the problems that I encountered when I interned at Seoul, which was complicated group dining process. I wanted to improve the user experience so that users could navigate and plan their group dining, but I quickly learned that designing the whole scenario was an ambitious project. I made LOTS of mistakes along the way such as choosing invisible color and overlooking the technical side of the mobile map. However, it was a great learning experience improving my design skills and getting feedback on my designs.
