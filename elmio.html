Discover a new relationship with El Mio.

El Mio is a personal assistant robot designed to take care of the user’s daily routine and relationships with friends and families.

Imagine your life with El Mio.

What if we had a Jarvis from the film Iron Man who could understand, help, and improve our lives and also be a friend? If you wish you could have one like him, here is El Mio, your personal assistant.

Every intormation that you get from El Mio will be filed in Application.

What's next?

The early A.I. assistant products came in a cylindrical form and they tend to become smaller and more personal.

User's Mobility & Personal Usage Needs

From research, A.I. speaker users are having in-home mobility and outdoor activity needs. Families with children have a desire to own their products separately. There are 62% of families with underage children, and they intend to purchase additional products because of the same needs.

HARDWARE DESIGN

Hologram technology

For nonverbal communications and friendly facial expressions, El Mio's facial part is crucial. Hologram technique would make El Mio communicate as if it is alive.

Physical sensor

Physical sensors like a 360 degrees distance sensor, a microphone, and a camera will let El Mio detect the user and interact with them.
