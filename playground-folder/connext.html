Pandemic Response Design Challenge 2020

Our team won first place in a hackathon PRDC2020! We are so proud of the result and the outcome that we got throughout a week.

Represented workspace

Connext is recreating workspace to the meta-space. Quick onboarding process leads you to go through building structure inside the building, go around virtual workspace where you can see who is staying at which part of the place.

Communicate, Work, Talk as We Did

Users can chat and talk with peers like they did in an actual room. Even in individual meeting room, or team collaboration room.

Digital Corkboard

Users can check, update and notice on the digital corkboard. This representation of physical workspace will be used for intuitive important announcement, OOO alert, etc.

Have a little fun with interactions

We added a little charm to this experience with 2 things! First one is sending a message and a random happy chat. Second one is customizing avatar.

Process

Even though it was designed in a short amount of time, we went though market research to find opportunity and conducted small interviews. Because of the foundation work, I think we could lend on the concept which make sense to a lot of remote workers.
