Link and Customize Your Robot Buddy!

For personalized assistants, El Mio is fully customizable. Application supports users to customize the look, voice, personality of the robot so that it can be your perfect robot buddy.

Watch Full Video ->

Your Buddy Needs to Be Real.

Nonverbal comunication is crucial for us human to feel the digital product is real. For the intimate interactions, El Mio's facial part is made by hologram technology. it makes communication as if it is alive. It has own hobby, lifestyle and emotional traits.

Life with El Mio

You can bring El Mio anywhere you want. Find adventure and live life together. Extra accessories like wheels, belts, rings will make this possible to bring El Mio everywhere!

Challenge

This personal project started with my own curiosity asking what will be the next generation personal robot. Because the early A.I. assistant products came in a cylindrical form and they tend to become smaller and more personal. I wanted to futurecast what could be next, and also how to make this relatable, also how to design interaction.

Industrial Design

Because of the needs that I got from research which is mobility needs, I made this to be small which has a part for facial expression and other part for sensors to fulfill the needs about in-home mobility and outdoor activity.

Mobile Interface Design

To make this experience full with customization and communication, mobile application was crucial for users to have. I designed the interface with bright color palette which represents bright, fun activity and memory you can make with El Mio.
